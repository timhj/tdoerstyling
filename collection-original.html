Things To Do In Surry Hills

Inner Sydney's hub of art, music, food & drink, Surry Hills is one of the rare trendy suburbs that the locals are happy to share the spoils with all who visit. A great spot to visit all year 'round, you can quite easily spend a full day taking in the sites, smells, sounds & tastes. Start with a morning coffee at one of the many cafes, cruise around the seemingly infinite art galleries, settle in for an afternoon drink at one of the many pubs or wine bars, then top it off with dinner or some live music.
