Chess in Hyde Park

Sydney chess scene is rocking

The chess tables and playing area in Hyde park exist for the weekend social player.

You can have a game on the life size board with human size (well child-size) chess pieces (A fun way to play chess with the kids) or bring your own chess pieces and sit down at one of the tables.

The chess sets are just behind the St James train station entrance on Elizabeth St (and Metro St James bar/restaurant) and are open when the suns out.
